# Prudentially Transient Abnegation of Life
This spell may be cast as an Instant action, even if another spell has been cast the same round, and targets either the caster or a willing visible target. It can only be used in immediate response to an injury that reduces the target to zero hit points. When it does, the target suffers dramatic and
ostentatiously obvious death, with mortal blows cleaving them asunder or gorily butchering them. They are dead to all mundane or magical examination. Up to two hours later however, at the
target’s discretion, the various remains of the target flow back together and restore their body with two hit points per caster level and two additional System Strain points gained. If the target’s remains are intentionally scattered widely, incinerated, or entirely eaten, however, they cannot
revive. The subject is aware of their surroundings while “dead”.
