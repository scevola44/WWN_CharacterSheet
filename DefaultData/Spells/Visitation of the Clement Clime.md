# Visitation of the Clement Clime
The caster and up to three visible allies per level are shielded from the excesses of ho stile
elemental energies, becoming immune to mundane extremes of heat and cold and natural acids or
electrical discharges. Against magical harm of this nature, they automatically take only half the
damage they normally would, or none if they make a successful Physical saving throw. This spell
lasts for one hour per level.
